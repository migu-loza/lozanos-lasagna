LASAGNA. THEN MORE.


In 1964, Mr. Lozano satisfied many guests at the Frensesco Hall. Second helpings were begged for as Mr. Lozano kept up with high demand of his specialty lasagna. He thought about the possibilities of serving his lasagna to a larger audience. His first restaurant, Lozano's Lasagna, was opened in Shelby, Michigan.


By 1972, Mrs. Lozano had joined the team with over 4 Lozano's Lasagna restaurants across West Michigan. She introduced her signature jelly filled empanadas to the menu.


In 1993, they opened their final restaurant in Muskegon, Michigan: number 9. Only 1 location (Downtown Muskegon) remains open after they decided to keep their focus on one restaurant. From lasagna and pizza to tostadas and desserts, Lozano's Lasagna serves people everyday (except Sunday) and is ready to make you smile :)
